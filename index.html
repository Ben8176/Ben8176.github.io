Potential Future Projects (VERY EXCITING)

Only projects under serious consideration / interest are listed. Mostly mechatronic and hardware based for now. Last updated 07/18/24

Electric Motorcycle

My electric bike is fine for commuting to campus (10 minutes) but I want more speed and power. I love sport motorbikes and I saw a full build of an electric motorcycle on youtube. It would be very fun to weld a frame but I'm also interested in building a carbon-fiber monocaque. In that case I wonder which parts of the bike would not be full carbon or fiberglass composite.
I would likely spend the most on the battery pack, so it's important to pick the right battery chemistry. LiPo for better surge power or lion for better energy density? Other cool features would be regen braking, launch mode, or building a custom motor controller board / firmware.


Quad/hexacopter with 4dof arm payload

Another one where I could just go insane on. Literally just a quadcopter with a 4+dof robot arm hanging underneath it. Would be extremely interesting to write the flight controller firmware for this as its akin to a pendulum system but with a shifting mass. Probably hundreds of commercial use cases, obviously the complexity could be pared down for most of them. I originally thought about this because I don't want my mom to fall off a ladder when stringing up christmas lights.


Robotic Welding System

I really like this idea because I think American manufacturing capacity needs to make a comeback and the only way to do this cheaply enough is to introduce heavy automation, see Hadrian, Rangeview, etc. You could do this a dumb and practical way with a gantry system similar to a 3d printer, but then again, arms are wayyyy cooler (and potentially more flexible for real world commercial deployments)
